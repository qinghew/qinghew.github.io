Qinghe Wang(王清和)

 

I am a first year PhD student at the IIAU-Lab in Dalian University of Technology, under the supervision of Prof. Huchuan Lu (卢湖川) and Xu Jia (贾旭). I obtained my Master's degree from Tianjin University supervised by Prof. Pengfei Zhu (朱鹏飞) and Bing Cao (曹兵). I was an intern at ByteDance Intelligent Creation Lab, Beijing in 2022. I obtained my Bachelor's degree from Dalian Maritime University. My main research interests includes Image/Video generation and editing with Diffusion models/GAN.
